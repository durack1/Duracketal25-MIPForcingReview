# canesm2 — CCCma (Canadian Centre for Climate Modelling and Analysis, Canada)

- **CMIP phase:** CMIP5 (historical, 1850–2005)
- **CMIP3 predecessor:** cccma_cgcm3_1 (CGCM3.1 T47 and T63 variants)
- **Model family / lineage:** CGCM3.1 (CMIP3) → CanESM2 (CMIP5). Full ESM with active carbon cycle: CanAM4 atmosphere + CTEM (Canadian Terrestrial Ecosystem Model) land carbon + CMOC (Canadian Model of Ocean Carbon) + CanOM4 ocean. CanAM4 uses prognostic bulk aerosol scheme (sulphate, BC, OC, sea salt, mineral dust) driven by Lamarque2010 emissions; ozone prescribed from Cionni2011 (no interactive chemistry).
- **Atmosphere / ocean components:** CanAM4 atmosphere (T63 spectral, ~2.8°, 35 levels) + CanOM4 ocean (NEMO-based) + CTEM land carbon + CMOC ocean carbon + sea-ice model
- **Primary documentation paper(s):** Arora, V.K., et al. (2011). Carbon emission limits required to satisfy future representative concentration pathways of greenhouse gases. *Geophys. Res. Lett.* 38(5), L05805. doi:10.1029/2010GL046270 [arora_carbon_2011 *(add)*] **[PDF: Aroraetal11GRL-CarbonEmissionLimitsRequiredToSatisfyRCPsCanESM2.pdf]**; VonSalzen, K., et al. (2013). ### Aerosol scheme context (Wilcox et al. 2013 + vonSalzen et al. 2013)
Wilcox, Highwood & Dunstone (2013, ERL 8:024033) Table 1: CanESM2 classified **SA** (first indirect effect = YES, second = NO). Confirmed by vonSalzen2013: (1) cloud droplet number Nc = 60(SO4)^0.2 (first indirect, p.107); (2) "the second indirect aerosol effect is not considered in the model" (p.109). All aerosol species (sulphate, BC, OC, sea salt, mineral dust) are prognostic bulk aerosols driven by emissions — NOT prescribed optical depths. Emissions for SO2, BC, OC from Lamarque et al. 2010.

### Open questions (remaining after vonSalzen2013 read)
1. **Land-use change (LU)**: GLC2000 confirmed for AMIP vegetation types (static). Time-varying Hurtt 2011 LU change forcing for coupled historical runs NOT addressed in vonSalzen2013 (AMIP paper). Needs Arora2011 or separate CanESM2 coupled-model documentation.
2. **N-deposition dataset**: Not addressed in vonSalzen2013 (atmospheric physics paper). Needs Arora2011 GRL or separate ESM documentation. Critical: GFDL ESMs used Lamarque 2013 ACCMIP; NCAR used Lamarque 2010.
3. **CO2 mode**: Concentration-driven (standard historical) vs emission-driven (esmHistorical) — depends on run type; not addressed in vonSalzen2013.
4. **Ocean C / DIC initialisation**: Not addressed in vonSalzen2013. Needs Christian et al. 2014 PDF in /resources.
