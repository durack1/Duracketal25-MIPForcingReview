### Table S1 conflicts found
- **None confirmed.** All Table S1 Y entries are correct. Prior candidate conflicts (BC=Y, OC=Y, SD=Y) RESOLVED by Meehl 2006: the 20C3M used the prognostic sulfur cycle (TV sulfate), time-evolving BC, and both are correctly coded Y. Prior Collins 2006 interpretation of FX-clim default applied to 20C3M was incorrect — 20C3M activated the prognostic option.

### Open questions
1. **OC dataset specifics**: Meehl 2006 §2a describes BC (population-scaled) but does not explicitly name the OC treatment. Collins et al. 2006b (CAM3 paper) would confirm the OC temporal treatment for 20C3M.
