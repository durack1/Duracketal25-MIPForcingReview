Key Findings
  • MIROC6, MIROC-ES2L, and MIROC-ES2H are fully standard across all 11 forcing categories — highest confidence in any CMIP6 model family.
  • GISS E2-1 family: LU inadvertently used CMIP5 cropland/pasture fractions (not LUH2) — discovered post-publication; ozone from GISS OMA offline chemistry (not CCMI).
  • CESM2/WACCM: ozone and volcanic aerosol bundled in a single file from the WACCM6 simulation (not CCMI ozone; VL not IACETH-SAGE3λ).
  • CNRM-CM6-1/HR: aerosols from TACTIC_v2 offline bundle (not MACv2-SP/Kinne 2013); LU from fixed ECOCLIMAP-II PD land cover — explicit rejection of LUH2.
  • ACCESS-ESM1-5: solar forcing carries +4.9 W m⁻² piControl offset; volcanic aerosol collapsed to 4 lat-bands with uniform AOD offset — both arise from CMIP5-era piControl mismatch carried into CMIP6.
  • 13 models have fully unknown (?) greenhouse gas status; 16 unknown solar; 14 unknown volcanic — documentation gaps concentrated in smaller modelling groups.
