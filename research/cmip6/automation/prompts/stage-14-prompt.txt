Document the climate forcing datasets used in two Australian CMIP6 models — ACCESS-CM2 (CSIRO-ARCCSS) and ACCESS-ESM1-5 (CSIRO) — for their CMIP6 `historical` experiment (1850–2014).

CONTEXT: Part of a forcing review of 64 CMIP6 models against the Eyring et al. 2016 (GMD doi:10.5194/gmd-9-1937-2016) standard protocol. Stage 14 of 17, Session 3 of 4.

KEY ARCHITECTURE:
- ACCESS-CM2: UM GA7.1 atmosphere (N96L85, 85 levels, top ~85km, ~135km) + NEMO3.6/ACCESS-OM2 ocean (1°) + CICE5.1 + CABLE2.0 land. AOGCM. **Uses UM GA7.1 = same atmospheric framework as HadGEM3-GC31-LL (Stage 11).** Primary paper: Bi et al. 2020 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2020MS002138).
- ACCESS-ESM1-5: UM GA1.0 atmosphere (N96L38, 38 levels, lower top ~40km, ~135km) + NEMO3.2 ocean (1°) + CICE5 + CABLE2.4-CN land BGC (active C-N cycle) + WOMBAT ocean BGC. Full ESM. **Older UM GA1.0, different from GA7.1.** Primary paper: Ziehn et al. 2020 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2019MS001829).

CRITICAL PRIOR-STAGE FINDING — ACCESS-CM2 / HadGEM3-GC31-LL SHARED ATMOSPHERE:
HadGEM3-GC31-LL (Stage 11) uses UM GA7.1 and was confirmed:
- O=✓std: CCMI prescribed (Hegglin 2016/UReading-CCMI-1-0) + GC3.1 ozone-redistribution scheme; Roberts 2019 verbatim
- Aerosols=✓std: GLOMAP-mode CEDS+BB4CMIP6 (NOT MACv2-SP — that is HighResMIP only); Mulcahy 2020 verbatim
- BC/OC=BB4CMIP6 ×2 per Johnson 2016 (published calibration)
- SO=✓std: Matthes 2017; VL=✓std: IACETH-SAGE3lambda (Thomason 2018 + Arfeuille 2014 lineage)
ACCESS-CM2 likely inherits this same forcing stack from UM GA7.1. Key question: does ACCESS-CM2 use the same GLOMAP-mode setup, or a different aerosol scheme (some ACCESS versions used CLASSIC aerosol)?

PRIOR-STAGE CROSS-CUTTING PATTERNS (Stages 1–13):
- UM GA7.1 forcing stack (HadGEM3-GC31-LL/-MM, KACE-1-0-G): O=✓std(CCMI+redistribution), GLOMAP-mode CEDS+BB4CMIP6 aerosols, Matthes 2017, IACETH-SAGE3lambda VL
- UM GA1 (old UKESM1-predecessor era): different aerosol scheme (CLASSIC), lower model top — different forcing dataset choices possible
- N-dep confirmed: NCAR-CCMI-2-0 for UKESM1 (Sellar 2019 Table A5 verbatim) — similar expected for ACCESS-ESM1-5 CABLE2.4-CN

CMIP6 STANDARD PROTOCOL (Eyring 2016):
- G: Meinshausen et al. 2017 (UoM-CMIP-1-2-0)
- O: Checa-Garcia et al. 2018 CCMI (UReading-CCMI-1-0)
- SD/BC/OC: CEDS (Hoesly 2018) + BB4CMIP6 (van Marle 2017) interactive; OR MACv2-SP (Stevens 2017) prescribed — both ✓std

- SO: Matthes et al. 2017 SOLARIS-HEPPA (SOLARIS-HEPPA-3-2)
- VL: IACETH-SAGE3lambda-3-0-0
