Document the climate forcing datasets used in five CMIP6 models — AWI-CM-1-1-LR (AWI), AWI-CM-1-1-MR (AWI), AWI-ESM-1-REcoM (AWI), INM-CM4-8 (INM), and INM-CM5-0 (INM) — for their CMIP6 `historical` experiment (1850–2014).

CONTEXT: Part of a forcing review of 64 CMIP6 models against the Eyring et al. 2016 (GMD doi:10.5194/gmd-9-1937-2016) standard protocol. Stage 15 of 17, Session 4 of 4.

KEY ARCHITECTURE:
- AWI-CM-1-1-LR: ECHAM6.3 atmosphere (T63L47, ~190km, 47 levels, top ~0.01 hPa) + FESOM2.0 ocean (unstructured mesh, ~120km avg). AOGCM. **ECHAM6.3 is the same atmospheric model as MPI-ESM1-2-LR (Stage 8).** Primary paper: Semmler et al. 2020 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2019MS002009).
- AWI-CM-1-1-MR: As AWI-CM-1-1-LR but with finer FESOM2.0 resolution (~25km avg). Atmosphere identical. All forcing expected IDENTICAL to LR.
- AWI-ESM-1-REcoM: AWI-CM-1-1 base (same ECHAM6.3 atmosphere) + REcoM2 ocean BGC (Regulated Ecosystem Model v2; includes Fe cycling). ESM with ocean BGC only. Primary paper: Lacroix et al. 2021 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2020MS002544).
- INM-CM4-8: INM atmosphere (2°×1.5°, L21, top ~10 hPa — LOW MODEL TOP) + INMOM ocean (0.5°). AOGCM. Russian Institute for Numerical Mathematics. Low top precludes good stratospheric resolution. Primary paper: Volodin et al. 2018 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2017MS001065) or Volodin et al. 2019.
- INM-CM5-0: INM atmosphere (2°×1.5°, L73, top ~0.2 hPa — HIGHER MODEL TOP than CM4-8, 73 levels) + INMOM ocean (0.5°). Same horizontal resolution as CM4-8 but greatly improved vertical resolution. Primary paper: Volodin et al. 2021.

CRITICAL PRIOR-STAGE FINDING — AWI-CM / MPI-ESM1-2-LR SHARED ATMOSPHERE:
MPI-ESM1-2-LR (Stage 8) uses ECHAM6.3 and was confirmed:
- O=✓std: CCMI prescribed on all levels; Keeble 2021 verbatim
- SD/BC/OC/SI=✓std: MACv2-SP prescribed aerosol (Stevens 2017); Mauritsen 2019 verbatim
- MD/SS=FXc: Kinne et al. 2013 fixed natural background
- LU=✓std: LUH2v2h (dynamic transitions)
AWI-CM likely inherits this forcing stack from ECHAM6.3. Test: does Semmler 2020 explicitly list the same CMIP6 forcing datasets?

CMIP6 STANDARD PROTOCOL (Eyring 2016):
- G: Meinshausen et al. 2017 (UoM-CMIP-1-2-0)
- O: Checa-Garcia et al. 2018 CCMI (UReading-CCMI-1-0)
- SD/BC/OC: CEDS (Hoesly 2018) + BB4CMIP6 (van Marle 2017) interactive; OR MACv2-SP (Stevens 2017) prescribed — both ✓std
- LU: Hurtt et al. 2020 LUH2 v2.1h (UofMD-landState-2-1-h)
- SO: Matthes et al. 2017 SOLARIS-HEPPA (SOLARIS-HEPPA-3-2)
- VL: IACETH-SAGE3lambda-3-0-0
