Document the climate forcing datasets used in five CMIP6 models — CAS-ESM2-0 (CAS), FGOALS-f3-L (CAS), FGOALS-g3 (CAS), FIO-ESM-2-0 (FIO-QLNM), and NESM3 (NUIST) — for their CMIP6 `historical` experiment (1850–2014).

CONTEXT: Part of a forcing review of 64 CMIP6 models against the Eyring et al. 2016 (GMD doi:10.5194/gmd-9-1937-2016) standard protocol. Stage 16 of 17, Session 4 of 4.

KEY ARCHITECTURE:
- CAS-ESM2-0: IAP AGCM5 atmosphere (T85, ~1.4°, L35, top ~2.2hPa) + CoLM4 land + LICOM3 ocean (1°×0.5°) + CICE4. Chinese Academy of Sciences. Primary paper: Zhang et al. 2020 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2020MS002210).
- FGOALS-f3-L: FAMIL3 atmosphere (C96, ~100km, L32, top ~2.16hPa) + CoLM land + LICOM3 ocean + CICE6. CAS/LASG. Primary paper: He et al. 2021 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2020MS002144).
- FGOALS-g3: GAMIL3 atmosphere (2°, L26, top ~36km) + CLM4 land + LICOM3 ocean + CICE6. CAS/LASG. Lower top than FGOALS-f3-L. Primary paper: Li et al. 2020 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2019MS002010).
- FIO-ESM-2-0: FOAM atmosphere (based on CAM5.3, T85L32) + POP2 ocean + MASNUM wave model + CICE5 + CLM4.5 land BGC. Chinese First Institute of Oceanography. ESM with ocean BGC and surface wave effects on mixing. Primary paper: Bao et al. 2020 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2019MS002014).
- NESM3: ECHAM6.3 atmosphere (T63L47, ~190km, top ~0.01hPa) + CLM4.5 land + NEMO3.4 ocean + CICE4.1. Nanjing University. **ECHAM6.3 = same atmospheric model as MPI-ESM1-2-LR (Stage 8) and AWI-CM (Stage 15).** Primary paper: Cao et al. 2018 (GMD doi:10.5194/gmd-11-2975-2018) — may need updated CMIP6 paper.

CRITICAL PRIOR-STAGE FINDING — NESM3 / MPI ECHAM6.3 CONNECTION:
MPI-ESM1-2-LR (Stage 8) uses ECHAM6.3 and was confirmed:
- O=✓std: CCMI prescribed; Keeble 2021 Table 1 verbatim
- Aerosols=✓std: MACv2-SP prescribed (Stevens 2017); Mauritsen 2019 verbatim
- SO=✓std: Matthes 2017; VL=✓std: IACETH-SAGE3lambda
AWI-CM (Stage 15) uses same ECHAM6.3 and was expected to inherit this stack. NESM3 also uses ECHAM6.3, so it may share this forcing — but NESM3 is built by a different institution (NUIST) and may configure forcing differently even with the same atmospheric code.

PRIOR-STAGE CROSS-CUTTING PATTERNS (Stages 1–15):
