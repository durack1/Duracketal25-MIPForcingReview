Document the climate forcing datasets used in six CMIP6 models — CIESM (THU), SAM0-UNICON (SNU), TaiESM1 (AS-RCEC), KIOST-ESM (KIOST), IITM-ESM (CCCR-IITM), and MCM-UA-1-0 (UA) — for their CMIP6 `historical` experiment (1850–2014).

CONTEXT: Part of a forcing review of 64 CMIP6 models against the Eyring et al. 2016 (GMD doi:10.5194/gmd-9-1937-2016) standard protocol. Stage 17 of 17 — FINAL STAGE. Session 4 of 4.

KEY ARCHITECTURE:
- CIESM: CIESM atmosphere (based on CAM5 with modifications, T85L30, top ~3hPa) + MOM6 ocean + CICE6 + CLM4.5 land. Tsinghua University, China. Primary paper: Lin et al. 2020 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2019MS002035).
- SAM0-UNICON: CAM5-UniCon atmosphere (T85L30, top ~3hPa, replaces CAM5 boundary layer with unified convection scheme) + POP2 ocean + CICE4 + CLM4.5 land. Seoul National University. Based on CESM1.3 framework. Primary paper: Park et al. 2019 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2018MS001519).
- TaiESM1: TaiESM1 atmosphere (based on CAM5.3/CESM1.3, T85L30, top ~3hPa, modified microphysics) + POP2 ocean + CICE4 + CLM4 land. Academia Sinica, Taiwan. Primary paper: Lee et al. 2020 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2020MS002198). Also Wang et al. 2021.
- KIOST-ESM: KIOST atmosphere (based on HadGEM2-AO, N96L38, top ~39km — OLD UM GA1 family, NOT GA7.1) + NEMO3.6 ocean + TOPAZ4 ocean BGC. Korea Institute of Ocean Science and Technology. ESM with ocean BGC. Primary paper: Pak et al. 2021 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2020MS002212).
- IITM-ESM: IITM atmosphere (based on SPEEDY T30L18 — COARSE, very simple spectral model, top ~10hPa) + MOM4p1 ocean + TOPAZ2 ocean BGC + CLM land. Indian Institute of Tropical Meteorology. ESM. Very simple/coarse model — simplified forcing expected. Primary paper: Swapna et al. 2018 (BAMS doi:10.1175/BAMS-D-14-00034.1); also Swapna et al. 2015.
- MCM-UA-1-0: MCM atmosphere (Minimum Complexity Model, T21L20 — VERY SIMPLE, spectral, 20 levels, top ~3hPa) + MOM5 ocean. University of Arizona. Highly simplified model — may use fixed or idealized forcings, not standard CMIP6 datasets. Primary paper: Stouffer et al. 2017 (J. Climate doi:10.1175/JCLI-D-17-0007.1) or Pollard & Reusch 2002.

CRITICAL PRIOR-STAGE FINDING — KIOST-ESM / HadGEM2 vs HadGEM3 DISTINCTION:
KIOST-ESM uses HadGEM2-AO atmospheric framework (old UK MetOffice GA1 lineage, L38, top ~39km), NOT UM GA7.1. This is a fundamentally different architecture from:
- HadGEM3-GC31-LL (Stage 11): UM GA7.1, L85, GLOMAP-mode, IACETH-SAGE3lambda (confirmed ✓std)
- ACCESS-CM2 (Stage 14): UM GA7.1, CABLE2.0 (confirmed/expected ✓std)
HadGEM2-AO ancestry means KIOST-ESM may use CLASSIC aerosol scheme (not GLOMAP-mode) and likely lacks the full CMIP6 aerosol-forcing infrastructure of GA7.1. CMIP5 HadGEM2 deviations included non-standard aerosol forcing — was this fixed for CMIP6 in KIOST-ESM?

PRIOR-STAGE CROSS-CUTTING PATTERNS (Stages 1–16):
- CAM5/CESM lineage (CIESM, SAM0-UNICON, TaiESM1): typically CEDS+BB4CMIP6 aerosols, CCMI ozone — but verify (CESM2 used WACCM-derived O; these are CESM1-based forks)
- Old UM GA1 family (KIOST-ESM, ACCESS-ESM1-5): lower model top, CLASSIC aerosol — different from GA7.1
- Very simple models (IITM-ESM, MCM-UA-1-0): may use fixed/simplified forcing rather than standard CMIP6 datasets

CMIP6 STANDARD PROTOCOL (Eyring 2016):
- G: Meinshausen et al. 2017 (UoM-CMIP-1-2-0)
- O: Checa-Garcia et al. 2018 CCMI (UReading-CCMI-1-0)
- SD/BC/OC: CEDS (Hoesly 2018) + BB4CMIP6 (van Marle 2017) interactive; OR MACv2-SP (Stevens 2017) prescribed — both ✓std

- SO: Matthes et al. 2017 SOLARIS-HEPPA (SOLARIS-HEPPA-3-2)
