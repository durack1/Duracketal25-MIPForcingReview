# CESM2-FV2 — National Center for Atmospheric Research, USA

- **CMIP phase:** CMIP6 (historical, 1850–2014)
- **CMIP5 predecessor:** CCSM4 (lower-res variant)
- **Model family / lineage:** CESM2 at lower resolution (FV2 = finite-volume ~2°). Atmospheric forcing expected IDENTICAL to CESM2.
- **Atmosphere / ocean components:** CAM6-FV2 / POP2 + CICE5; same components as CESM2 at coarser resolution
- **Primary documentation paper(s):** Danabasoglu et al. 2020, *JAMES* doi:10.1029/2019MS001916 [danabasoglu_cesm2_2020]

**CESM2/CESM2-FV2 forcing identity**: FV2 differs from CESM2 only in horizontal resolution; all atmospheric forcing expected identical.
