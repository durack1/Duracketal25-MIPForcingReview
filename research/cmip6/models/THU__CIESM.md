# THU CIESM
## Architecture
CIESM atmosphere (based on CAM5, T85L30, top ~3hPa) + MOM6 ocean + CICE6 + CLM4.5 land. Tsinghua University, China. Primary paper: Lin et al. 2020 (JAMES doi:10.1029/2019MS002035).
