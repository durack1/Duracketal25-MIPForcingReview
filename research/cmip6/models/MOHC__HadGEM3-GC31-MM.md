# HadGEM3-GC31-MM — MOHC+NERC (UK)

- **CMIP phase:** CMIP6 (historical, 1850–2014)
- **Model family / lineage:** Higher-resolution variant of HadGEM3-GC31-LL. UM GA7.1 atmosphere (N216L85, ~60km, 85 levels) + NEMO3.6 (ORCA025, 0.25°) + CICE6 + JULES. Same component structure and forcing as HadGEM3-GC31-LL; resolution only differs.
- **Primary documentation paper(s):** Roberts et al. 2019, *GMD* doi:10.5194/gmd-12-4999-2019 (covers both LL and MM)

## Forcing datasets — all identical to HadGEM3-GC31-LL

Roberts et al. 2019 covers both LL and MM in the same paper; Walters et al. 2019 GA7.1 applies to both. All forcing datasets confirmed identical to HadGEM3-GC31-LL.
